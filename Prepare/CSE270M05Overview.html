CSE 270: W05 Overview

Quality assurance (Q/A) is the process of determining if a product meets quality standards. The Q/A process is integrated in all stages of the software development; quality is not something that can be "tacked onto" the end of a project.

Objectives

By the end of the week, each student will be able to:

  • Define quality assurance.
  • Identify the key activities involved in providing Q/A assurances to a project.
  • For a given project, identify the key quality metrics.
  • Collect quality data and report on your findings.

Plan of the Week

This week, we will complete the following activities:

  1. Reading: Read one article.
  2. Define Quality. Define the quality requirements for one of your phone's sensors and create a test plan.
  3. Conduct your experiments. Conduct the experiments listed in you test plan. Carefully record what you have learned.
  4. Report. Report on what you have learned. Does your phone work the way you expected it to work?

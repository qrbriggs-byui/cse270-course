CSE 270: W14 Overview

If enjoy the process of getting to the bottom of mysteries and figuring out how things work, then a career in testing may be the thing for you. The questions remains: where to you go from here? What types of jobs are available? What does a testing career path look like? This lesson will help to answer these questions.

If you do not want to be a tester, you will still find yourself working with them throughout your career. This lesson will help you understand testers better and appreciate their contribution to the development team.

Objectives

By the end of the week, each student will be able to:

  • Describe different career paths for testers.

Plan of the Week

This week, we will complete the following activities:

  1. Reading: Read one chapter.
  2. Research Jobs. On the internet, research three testing jobs. Report on them on a discussion board.
  3. Report. Write a report summarizing the class' findings on testing jobs.

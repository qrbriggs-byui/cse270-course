CSE 270: W02 Overview

This week we will learn about the process of testing something. We will accomplish this by doing a little testing of our own.

Objectives

By the end of the week, each student will be able to:

  • Gather information about a given domain for the purpose of testing.
  • Create a simple test plan and conduct it.
  • Learn a little about various testing organizations and associations.

Plan of the Week

This week, we will complete the following activities:

  1. Reading. Complete the reading materials.
  2. Find three sources. Find three sources describing how your camera's auto-focus system works.
  3. Learn about a testing organization. Visit the web site of a testing organization to learn about the profession and the services they provide.
  4. Join a group. From the Icebreaker activity, find classmates with whom you would like to work this semester.
  5. Share Information. Report on what you have learned about your camera's auto-focus system. See what other people have learned about the same camera.
  6. Create a test plan. Create a list of experiments that you can do on your own camera to verify that the auto-focus system works the way you expect.
  7. Conduct your experiments. Conduct the experiments listed in you test plan. Carefully record what you have learned.
  8. Report. Report on what you have learned. Does your camera work the way you expected it to work?

Assignments

  • Teach: Lab: Auto-Focus

CSE 270: W03 Overview

This week we will learn about how testing activities are performed within various software development methodologies.

Objectives

By the end of the week, each student will be able to:

  • Summarize how Testing fits into different S/W Development Lifecycles.

Plan of the Week

This week, we will complete the following activities:

  1. Reading: Read three chapters and two articles.
  2. Select: Meet with your partner(s) and select a software development lifecycle model.
  3. Research: With your partner(s), research how the selected software lifecycle model works.
  4. Create: With your partner, write a “personal plan” of how you will perform your testing duties in that lifecycle model.

Assignments

  • Teach: Lab: Auto-Focus

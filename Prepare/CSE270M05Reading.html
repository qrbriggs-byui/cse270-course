CSE 270: W05 Reading: Quality Assurance

The reading activity for this week includes reading one article and downloading an app to your phone.

A Review of Software Quality Models

Please download and read the following article:

J. Miguel et al. "A Review of Software Quality Models for the Evaluation of Software Products," International Journal of Software Engineering & Applications, vol. 5, no. 6, pp. 31-54, Nov. 2014, [Online] Available: http://airccse.org/journal/ijsea/papers/5614ijsea03.pdf

As you read this article, notice the many components of quality. One of your first tasks for this week's lab will be to identify the quality levels for your phone's display.

Sensor App

Download a sensor app that will show the different sensor available on your phone.

  • Android: On Google Play, search for Sensor Lab, Phone Check and Test, or TestM.
  • iOS: On the Apple app store, search for Phone Diagnostics or TestM.

Any of these apps will work for this week's lab.

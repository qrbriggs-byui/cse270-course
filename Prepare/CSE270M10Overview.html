CSE 270: W10 Overview

Architecture testing is the process of testing an application system-wide. In other words, this is a holistic view of the system (as opposed to integration testing or unit testing which focuses on a small sub-set of the system). One large component of architecture testing is usability testing. This involves evaluating the product from the user's perspective, usually by observing representative users interact with the system. The focus of our lesson this week will be how to conduct good usability studies.

Objectives

By the end of the week, each student will be able to:

  • Define usability testing.
  • Create a usability test plan.
  • Conduct a usability study.

Plan of the Week

This week, we will complete the following activities:

  1. Reading: Read one chapter.
  2. Identify an Application: With your partner(s), identify a mobile application suitable for this week's lab.
  3. Usability Plan: With your partner(s), create a usability plan.
  4. Usability Study: With your partner(s), conduct a usability test (sometimes called a usability study).

CSE 270: W04 Reading: Testing Techniques

A seasoned tester utilizes various testing techniques during a single software development project. The more techniques a tester has mastered, the more effective they can be. The purpose of this lesson is to learn about several techniques.
This week, we will receive a testing technique assignment from the course instructor and gather information about your testing technique.

Research

With your partner(s), find a few sources describing your assigned testing technique. You will need at least four "high-quality" sources.
The majority of your reading will be researching a testing technique. Please work with your partner from last week to research your assigned topic and present it. Find four high-quality sources describing your testing technique. The BYU-I Library's Guide may help you in this process. The best way to conduct this research is as follows:

  1. Start with an initial and untrustworthy source like Wikipedia. An initial source like this will give you an overview of the topic and probably not contain too many factual errors.
  2. Look at the References and External Links section of the primary source. These will often refer to a more trustworthy source you can cite.
  3. Find keywords and phrases in the primary source. Search in the Library or for quality sources using these keywords and phrases.
  4. Continue this process until you have found quality sources on all aspects of the topic. You can expect to read a dozen or so articles until you settle on the half dozen or so you will need.

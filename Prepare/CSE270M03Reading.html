CSE 270: W03 Reading: Software Methodologies

Testing activities play a significant role in each phase of the software construction process. Through the years, many different software construction processes have been developed. These processes are called "Software Methodologies" or "Software Development Lifecycle Models." This week, we will learn the tester's role in the various software development methodologies.

The reading for this week consists of three chapters: Life Cycle-Based Testing, The Software Development Process, and Testing Through the Software Lifecycle. Additionally, there are two articles to be read.

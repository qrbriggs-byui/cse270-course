CSE 270: W10 Lab: Usability Study

Our lab this week will be to conduct a usability study utilizing the usability plan previously created.

Conduct the Study

Each member of the team will interview three people. Detailed data from these interviews will be collected and compiled into a single report.

The combination of all this data should address the question: will the average user be able to accomplish the task that the feature was designed to facilitate? This question should be answered with data collected during the study. Make sure that all your conclusions are fully supported by the collected data.

Submission

There will be one submission for the entire group. This submission will consist of all the data collected during the study, and any conclusions that could be drawn.

The size of your report will vary. Most teams will have one page per interview with an additional page to summarize their findings.

CSE 270 Teach Lab M14: Testing as a Career

Lab 14 - Career Move

Overview

You have been working with the test team for the last 5 years. You have found a new opportunity within the company to be a Test Lead or Software Developer.

Assignment

Use what you have learned from this course and the reading to answer the following issues.

  • Identify your current responsibilities. How would you transfer those responsibilities over to other team members?
    • What type of skills, experiences, and projects for a tester would be good on a resume?
  • How long do you think the transition would take, what are complete, and what milestones signify completion and close-out? Create a schedule.

Submission

Post your transition plan.

CSE 270 Teach Lab M10: Verification and Validation

Lab 10 - Presentation to Customer

Overview

Making sure that the customer gets the right product is just as important as the product working correctly. Both are part of Testing.

Assignment

Based on the information you have tested, create a presentation to present to a product customer.

Progress Report

  • Identify three tested aspects of Dr. Mobile's software.
  • Describe the expected feature.
  • How was it tested?

Current Issues

  • Include issues (at least 3) currently being addressed (bugs) by the test in your presentation.

Present information

  • Record your presentation or Present your report to another group.
  • Post your presentation information.

Record and Report on issues found

  • Record any issues found from a classmate's presentation and post them as a reply to their presentation post. You can't reply to a classmate that has already received a reply.

Create Resolution Plan

  • Identify items that need to be addressed and create a plan on how to complete them. If no replies, then create 3 issues and include the resolution to your plan.

Submission

Post presentation(original post), recorded issues (replies to another team), and resolution plan (reply to self)to the discussion board.

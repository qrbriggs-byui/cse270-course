CSE 270: W10 Notes

Though usability studies are usually performed by usability engineers, all testers should be able to perform a simple usability test.

Note that many students will have taken or will take CSE 471 where this will be discussed in much more detail.

Prepare Yourself

Nothing special this week.

Activities

Activity: Reading

Some teams may need help with their project selection. Expect to answer a few e-mails on this topic.

Activity: Plan

Online: As with several earlier weeks, reach out to each team individually. It would be best if you could arrange a synchronous meeting. If not, then answer their questions via e-mail.

Campus: The first day, talk about creating a usability test. Spend most of the day in lab time where the teams discuss their plans.

Campus: The second day, talk about interviewing people and observing. Spend most of the day in lab time where the teams refine plans.

Activity: Lab

Each student needs to conduct their plan with real people. Make sure this is done. As with previous submissions, hold the students to a high quality bar.

Announcements

Two or three stories of usability studies you have conducted or observed.

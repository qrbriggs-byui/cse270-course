CSE 270: W13 Notes

I have worked on teams that were a well-oiled machines. I have also worked with a bunch of cowboys. The purpose of this lesson is to help students appreciate the benefits of working on well-oiled machines and to recognize what qualities in a team make it that way.

Note that we have an entire course on CMM: CSE 473 Process Improvement. Students who are interested in this or want to learn more should be encouraged to take that course.

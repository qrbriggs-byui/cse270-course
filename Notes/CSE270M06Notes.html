CSE 270: W06 Notes

This week, the focus is on automation tools. Though I am sure you used different tools in your job, our emphasis this week is that the students get hands-on experience with some tool.

Prepare Yourself

You might need to spend an hour or so installing Selenium and familiarizing yourself with it.

Activities

Activity: Reading

Perhaps pursue the other Selenium pages on this site in case you need to refer a team to some of this documentation.

Activity: Lab

The vast majority of your time this week will be spent answering student's questions about Selenium. Expect to spend a lot of time on e-mail.

Online: Try to be super responsive with their questions on e-mail.

Campus: Prepare a quick demonstration in class and have the students follow along. Spend the remainder of the class time in a lab format where the students work with their partner(s) and you circulate through the room answering questions.

Grading should be super easy. Does the screen-shot represent a working solution or not?

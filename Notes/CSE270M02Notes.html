CSE 270: W02 Notes

This week is all about giving the students some hands-on experience with testing. We will learn how to be more rigorous later in the semester.

Prepare Yourself

Perform the lab yourself. Be prepared to answer the student's questions about the lab instructions and what they can expect to find. These questions are best answered with "When I did this..."

Activities

Activity: Reading

There might be some student questions about what constitutes a good source for how their camera works.

Activity: Group Signup

Both campus and face-to-face students will need to sign up in groups.

Online: Have the students self-select. You might need to reach out to some of the students or even enroll them in a group. Make sure the group size is 2-3 students.

Campus: This is best done in class. Bring up the "People" tab and drag students into various groups. Give the students a chance to name their group.

Activity: Lab

Periodically monitor the discussion board. Give students feedback when their post is off topic or of insufficient quality.

Announcements

Consider creating an announcement describing your experience with this lab.

This will be another great time for a "tales from the trenches" story.

Scenerio

Dr Mobil Health Device

Company:

Your have just been hired by H.E.L.P, Inc (Health Emergency Lifeline Protectors) to start up and oversee the new test team for their latest product line: Dr Mobil.

Dr Mobil is a fictious device that will help EMP(Emergency Medical Personnel) in a medical emergency to diagnose the injured. The device is handheld, about the size of an iPad. It is made out of clear plastic carbonate, with two handles on either side.

This device will have all of the current sensors found in today’s devices:

  • Internal focus Sensors: Accelerator, Gyroscope, InclineMeter, Oscilloscope, gForce
  • External focus Sensors:
    • Camera: Spectrograph, Color detection, Light meter
    • Barometer, Proximeter, Magnet Meter, Compass, GPS,
    • Sound: Mic, Sound meter, Tone Detector
    • Spectrum Analyzer
    • Touch: Finger Print, User Input, Tap
    • Radio: FM/AM
    • Network: Cellular, Wireless, Bluetooth
  • Emitters: Speaker, Infrared, Light

Additional features of the product:

  • Uses latest solar power technology, using the clear glass as an energy collection point from the sun, and storing it in 24 hour batteries.
  • Can communicate via Satellite, Ham Radio, Tower to Tower (Relay, Cell or Ham)
  • Communicate via external sensors
    • Wrist band – pick biometrics, monitoring, etc.
    • Other devices: Reading information that is needed to be broadcast
    • 360 degrees of sensory of temperature change, depth of 25 feet through solid rock (IF Sensor)

Communication:

  • Communication with main office (Cell Phone)
  • Communication with other medical devices (Point to Point)
  • Communication with base camp (Wireless network)
  • Communication with supply chain (Satellite Phone)
  • Communication with transport (Short wave radio)

This device will be used during disaster (natural and manmade) scenarios.
Software Application Functionality

  • Determine biometrics: temp, pulse, pressure, oxygen level, diseases,
  • Wrist band – pick biometrics, monitoring, etc.

Software capabilities:

  • EMP will be able to communicate with doctors in remote locations, such as home base.
  • EMP can use the device in search and resue.
  • EMP can use it to collect vitals, examine patients
  • EMP can attach an arm bracelet to continue monitoring patients while attending to other patients.
  • EMP can arrange transport of patients with local vehicles
  • EMP can communicate via different channels (Cell, Ham, and Satellite)
  • EMP can arrange supply needs, via supply chain.
  • EMP can communicate with base camp.
  • EMP can communicate with other local medical devices to share information
  • EMP can recieve updated information such a status, weather, and other relevant information.

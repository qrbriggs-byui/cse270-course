CSE 270 Teacher Notes M05: Quality Assurance Measurements

Week 05: Instructor Notes

Overview

Testing is an integral part of the Quality Assurance process, so much so that many companies confuse the difference between the two. Students will understand the differences between QA and Testing.

Objectives

By the end of the week, each student will be able to:

  • Measure Quality, Maturity, Process Improvement, Root Cause and Measurement with regards to Testing

Partnership/Group work:

  • No partnership or group work is specified, but students can work together and turn in individual assignments.

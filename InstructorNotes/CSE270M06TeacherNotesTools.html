CSE 270 Teacher Notes M06: Weekly Topic

Week 06 Instructor Notes

Overview

Students will be looking at different Testing Management Tools and Testing software libraries.

Objectives

By the end of the week, each student will be able to:

  • Understand the critical role testing has in DevOps
  • Evaluate Testing Tools

CSE 270: W14 Research: Testing Careers

A good way to understand a career path in a given area is to look at the various types of job that are available. To help us understand testing career paths, we will start by researching testing jobs.

Note that this is an individual assignment. Post your findings on a discussion board for the entire class to see.

Three Jobs

Find three job openings for testers on the internet. You can look at specific companies (such as Google or Apple) or you can look at job placement services (like Monster). Make sure that the jobs openings you found were not previously found by your classmates.

Summarize Each

Once you found three jobs, then summarize what they entail. These summaries should include the following, but you may wish to add additional information:

  • Job title
  • Salary
  • Required education
  • Required certifications
  • Required technology skills
  • Duties and responsibilities
  • Link to the job opening

Report

Report on your findings in this discussion board. Create a unique thread for each job opening you found. Make sure that the job opening was not previously reported by your classmates!
